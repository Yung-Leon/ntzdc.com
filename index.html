LIVELIHOOD

NTZDC is mandated to protect Kenya’s natural forests through the establishment of tea buffer zones. These buffer zones not only prevent forest encroachment but also provide an alternative source of livelihood for communities living near forested areas. The corporation also manages commercial te a production and conservation initiatives that contribute to Kenya’s economy and environmental sustainability.
